# Privacy Policy for Expense Tracker App

Last updated: November 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Expense Tracker app (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles your data.

## 1. Information We Collect
The app does **NOT** collect or share any personal information.

All data (expenses, notes, dates, recurring status) is stored **locally on your device only** using secure local storage (AsyncStorage).

We do not collect:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Financial or banking details
- Device information

## 2. How We Use Data
All expense data is used **only within your device** to display and manage your expense history.  
No information is uploaded to our servers.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
Your expense data is stored securely within your phone storage using AsyncStorage.  
We do not have access to your data.

If you uninstall the app, all stored data will be deleted permanently.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The app does **not** use any third-party analytics, tracking, ads, or external APIs.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
The app does not target or collect information from children under 13.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be available on this page.
